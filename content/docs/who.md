### Primary Audience
These resources are for you if:
- You live in a historic property in Edinburgh (especially if your property is listed or located in a conservation area); AND
- You own the property you are living in; AND
- You are considering retrofitting your windows.

### Secondary Audience
Although these resources are primarily for historic property owner-occupiers in Edinburgh, you may still benefit from using these materials if you are:
- A **tenant** of historic property in Edinburgh/Scotland;
- A **landlord** of historic property in Edinburgh/Scotland;
- A **non-for-profit organisation** that interacts with residents of historic properties in Edinburgh/Scotland: such as community councils, heritage organisations, street associations, or retrofit collectives;
- A **property professional** who advises owners of historic properties in Edinburgh/Scotland: such as estate agents, factors, or landlord associations
